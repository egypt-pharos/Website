Who We Are

We are Egypt Pharos, a robotics team inspired by the engineering marvels of ancient Egypt. We bridge the gap between software and hardware, mastering Python, C++, OpenCV, AI Modeling and Electronics.

In 2025, we dominated the national stage, securing 1st Place in the RCJ Rescue Line category at the Egypt National Competition.

We also showcased superior collaboration skills, earning 1st Place in the SuperTeam challenge.

Global Impact

In 2024, Egypt Pharos took the stage among the world's elite.

We proudly represented Egypt at the International RoboCup Junior (RCJ) finals in Eindhoven, Netherlands. Competing against top-tier global teams pushed our engineering limits and solidified our place in the international robotics community.
